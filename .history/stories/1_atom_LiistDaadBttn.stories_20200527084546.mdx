## Properties

- **`type`**: possible values: `location` or `filtering`.
- **`size`**: possible values `big` (default) or `small`.
- **`boxshadow`**: boolean, if attribute is present boxshadow is applied.
- **`nSelectedFilters`**: `if nSelectedFilters > 0` a badge is displayed that indicates the amount of filters applied.
